The full prompt library — copy and use

A reusable set of prompts for every stage of the SDLC, tagged by role. Fill in the [BRACKETED] parts, treat every output as a first draft, and never paste confidential or production data.

Prompt in 4 parts Context — system & constraints/ Role — expertise to adopt/ Task — one ask + format/ Check — surface assumptions, then verify

Do

  • Treat every output as a first draft and review it.
  • Give rich context: code snippets, requirements, examples.
  • Iterate in follow-ups to refine the result.
  • Ask it to surface its assumptions and risks.
  • Use it to learn: “explain this code, or this error.”

Don’t

  • Enter confidential, client, personal, or production data or credentials.
  • Ship code or specs you don’t understand.
  • Trust facts without verifying against a source of truth.
  • Treat it as a system of record.
  • Assume it knows internal or proprietary systems.

Enterprise guardrails

Use only approved tools and accounts. Never enter confidential, personal, or regulated data. AI assists, and humans stay accountable for every output. When unsure, check with your team lead or risk & compliance.

Tool note: Claude suits long documents and large-context reasoning; ChatGPT suits quick snippets and brainstorming. Both follow the same rules, so use whichever is on your approved-tools list.
